TxForge does not collect, store, or process any personal information. All operations happen locally in your browser.

2. Wallet Connection

Your wallet connection and transaction signing happens directly between your browser and the blockchain network. We never see your private keys or wallet data.

3. Analytics

We may use basic analytics to understand usage patterns, but no personally identifiable information is collected.

We may use browser local storage for app preferences only. No tracking cookies are used.
